Exclude faces that are not visible. To do this, compute the outward normal to each face, and exclude any face whose normal has a positive z-component. To find the outward normal to a face, choose any three consecutive vertices of the face a, b, c; the cross-product (c - b) x (a - b) is the outward normal.
